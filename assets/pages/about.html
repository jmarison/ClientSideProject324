About Us

Here's a Q&A with some questions from users

What is FitCal?

FitCal is a fitness application meant to get you on track and hold you to your goals. We personlize a plan for you based on your goals and statistics.

How is my plan made?

We take simple facts about you: Age, weight, etc. along with the weight loss or gain goals you are trying to achieve, and formulate the perfect plan for ataining your dream body.

What motavated this site's creation?

Here at FitCal, we've grown up around increasing obesity rates; not just in America, but around the world. It's out mission to try and provide a way to get society back on track with their health.

How is my data used?

Your data is secure with us. You can trust that your data will not be sold, and the demographical data that we collect is fully anonymized.

Why FitCal?

FitCal is designed to be the Fitness Application for everybody, so it's design is kept simple. We echoed this in the name: It's Fitness through the counting of Calories!
